Day 1: Land at Liberia Airport

Arrive around 3pm, go through customs, get the rental car, check in to the Hilton Garden Inn. Hang out by the pool, eat dinner, and sleep. If it's early enough, drive to Playa Hermosa and explore the beach.

Next morning we head to Monteverde, stopping to swim at a waterfall.

Day 2: To Monteverde via Catarata Llanos del Cortés

Wake up, eat a leisurly breakfast at the hotel, and drive a half hour to Catarata (waterfall) Llanos del Cortés for a relaxing swim.

Afterwards, find a place for lunch, and head to Monteverde.
