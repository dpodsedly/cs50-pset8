Day 1: Arrive at Liberia Airport

Land around 3pm, go through customs, get the rental car, check in to the Hilton Garden Inn. Hang out by the pool, eat dinner, and sleep. If it's early enough, drive to Playa Hermosa and explore the beach.

Next morning we head to Monteverde, stopping to swim at a waterfall.

Day 2: To Monteverde via Catarata Llanos del Cortés

Wake up, eat a leisurly breakfast at the hotel, and drive a half hour to Catarata (waterfall) Llanos del Cortés for a relaxing swim.

Afterwards, find a place for lunch, and head to Monteverde.

Day 3: Villa El Sol and Cloud Forest Hike

Breakfast at Villa El Sol, then hiking in the cloud forest. Lunch, and hang out in the pool, then explore Santa Elena town a find a place to eat some Costa Rican food for dinner.

Day 4: Ziplining!

Spend the day at Sky Adventures. Zip line course in the morning, then lunch, followed by hiking the hanging bridges.

Day 5: To Manuel Antonio

Leisurely morning, drive to Manuel Antonio and check into Casa Gina. Stop to see the crocodiles in the Tarcoles river.

Day 6: Manuel Antonio National Park

Early morning guided tour in the national park to look for sloths, monkeys, and other animals. Lunch and swim at Manuel Antonio beach.

Day 7 - 10: Beach, pool, day trip to Nauyaca Waterfall

Lounge by the pool, swim and snorkel at the beach, explore the town, and squeeze in a morning trip to Nauyaca Waterfall.
